Welcome to Your ealth Center

Hello everyone, our team has created this website to solve a very big problem. And that is the problem, that when the second wave of covid came, then many people could not arrange beds in hospitals and many people did not even know that ,in which hospital beds,ICU, arrangements are there? And which hospital has how many ICU and beds? And if the bed is known, then it is not known ,how many beds are vacant in which hospital? Due to which many people have lost their lives, because they had to go to every hospital and ask whether there is a bed or ICU arrangement or not, and it gets too late. Due to which the patient dies.

So to solve this problem our team has created this website. So that sitting at home, we will be able to see on Google which hospital has beds and ICU facilities, and which hospital has empty beds. With this it will be that the patient will not have to wait much and he will be easily arranged for beds or ICU. And the patient's life will also be saved.
